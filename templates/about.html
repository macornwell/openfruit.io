Why

Understanding how fruits perform in the real world can be challenging and is an on-going experiment for many. Traditional sources of information do not provide any level of insight of how fruits are going to perform for us on a localized basis.

OpenFruit seeks to utilize the power of digital communities to provide a common grounds for fruit information.

OpenFruit

  1. Keep track of important information. Like your growing statistics.
  2. A current and follow on generation who will know and understand fruiting and useful plants, better than any human before them. With some work, in a fraction of the time. How:By giving humans, to including ourselves, access to most intelligent fruit science database and aggregated system ever built.
  3. A platform for others to build upon. The platform allows creativity to use the available data for overall betterment. How:Linking together living data. People, events and life.
  4. Be able to compare if what you do, is what you should be doing. How:By being able to examine what data show.
